Feeling overwhelmed yet with all this band stuff? Well, do not fear quite yet. Luckily for you, a convenient digital musical dictionary can be found here to make your journey a lot easier!

Musicality The portrayal quality of the musical piece
Dynamics How loud or soft the music is
Conductor The person directing the band
Section A group of people playing a specific instrument
In-Tune Playing within the correct pitch
Tone The quality of the individual instrument sound
Lip-Up Phrase telling musicians to play in a sharper pitch
Lip-Down Phrase telling musicians to play in a flatter pitch
Melody Most important phrase in the music
Mute A device used to muffle the sound of an instrument
Pitch The highness or lowness of a musical sound
Metronome A device used to keep a steady beat
Tempo The speed of the music
Section Leader The principal player of a section
Accent Emphasis on a note or phrase
Mouthpiece Object that is blown into to create sound
Reed Piece of thin wood that vibrates to create sound
Bell The end of an instrument where sound comes out
Composer Someone who writes music
Genre The different types of music
